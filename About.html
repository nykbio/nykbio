About Us

We are a forward-thinking organization led by experienced technocrats and business development professionals. With extensive expertise in the pharmaceutical industry, preclinical testing, medical devices, and drug development, we specialize in: Biocompatibility studies in compliance with ISO requirements.

Our Team of experienced professionals have submitted reports in compliance with CDSCO-India / EU MDR / 510k USFDA / PMDA/ KFDA/ SFDA/ Anvisa / MEDSAFE.

Also we are team of experienced professionals in the domain of Drug discovery and regulatory compliance which include preclinical research, toxicological testing, clinical research and strategic patent grants required for IND application submission to DCGI for further clinical trials, CDSCO approval for its market readiness.

As part of our growing global portfolio, we also work closely with esteemed clients to facilitate clinical trials required for regulatory approvals. Our focus is on seamless execution, strict adherence to compliance, and delivering high-quality results.

We also assist medical device companies with Chemical characterization , Toxicological risk assessments, Comprehensive support for testing in line with international regulatory standards.

Our team has been supporting over 25 manufacturers in India and abroad, providing tailored solutions for their biocompatibility and testing needs.

Apart from all these domains we are also closely associated with domestic drug manufactures, and assist in contract manufacturing and marketing of these drug.

We also cover assistance in pharmaceutical testing lab set up, operation, instrumentation, highly specialized human resource deployment, regulatory approvals and business development.
